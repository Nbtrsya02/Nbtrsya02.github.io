OUR STORY

Hair Studio.

Welcome to HAIR STUDIO., In the search of 20 years collective trichological experience, we are now providing tailor made treatment for hair and scalp conditions ranging from genetic & nutritional related hair loss, alopecia areata and scarring alopecia to psoriasis, severe scaling and flaking of the scalp, also for cosmetic damage and overly-processed hair.In the blend of the very traditional philosophy with advance technologies, we would like to provide our services that enhance our clients' physical appearance, individual personalities and also mental relaxation.
